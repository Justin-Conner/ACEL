The begining

Acel IT Solutions (formally pcCashdrawer) was founded in 1985 with the purpose of providing best-in-class system and digital signage solutions for all types of retail environments. We started as the company that arrived on-site with RVs fully loaded with the right equipment for the job. Today, we continue to deliver exceptional white-glove service to the biggest retailers around the country.
